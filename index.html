Individual Components Details

Detailed overview of each team member's specialized contribution to the BotanyBuddy system.

Pathirana K.P.V

IT21244766

Automated Disease Identification System

Developing an automated disease identification system for Anthurium, Mango, Bell Pepper, and Durian plants using advanced image recognition algorithms and machine learning. The system addresses challenges in providing consistent plant care guidance during peak times by automating diagnosis processes and delivering actionable recommendations without direct staff intervention.

Focus: Image Recognition ‚Ä¢ Disease Detection ‚Ä¢ Post-Purchase Support

Bandara A.M.B.S

IT21229398

Predictive Sales Analytics System

Implementing time-series analysis and machine learning models including Random Forest and LSTM to predict sales patterns and identify seasonal trends for Mango, Durian, and Anthurium plants. This data-driven approach enables nursery owners to optimize inventory management, predict peak buying seasons, and implement targeted marketing strategies.

Focus: Machine Learning ‚Ä¢ Sales Prediction ‚Ä¢ Inventory Management

Theekshana K.T

IT21280160

Quality Assessment & Farmer Network Platform

Creating a comprehensive platform combining plant quality assessment tools with social networking features for nursery owners. The system evaluates nutrient deficiency, physical damage, and environmental stress for mango, bell pepper, durian, and anthurium plants while connecting farmers based on location, climate, and crop specialization for knowledge sharing.

Focus: Quality Assessment ‚Ä¢ Social Networking ‚Ä¢ Farmer Collaboration

Gamlath W.A.V.K

IT21211928

Plant Age Calculation & QR-Based Guidance System

Developing a web-based system that calculates plant age and provides customized, age-specific care instructions for Anthurium, Durian, Bell Pepper, and Mango plants. The system uses image analysis and data insights to predict plant developmental stages and automate care recommendations, reducing staff workload while enhancing customer experience through accessible guidance.

Focus: Age Prediction ‚Ä¢ QR Technology ‚Ä¢ Automated Care Instructions
